2021 Governors Election Results

In California, incumbent Gov. Gavin Newsom prevailed in an off-year recall on September 14 over a wide field of challengers after portraying the recall effort as a GOP power grab. Meanwhile, regularly scheduled gubernatorial elections will happen in New Jersey and Virginia on November 2. Check out this page for the live stream on Twitch of these results at 11:00 PM EDT (8:00 PM PDT) on September 14 and 7:00 PM EDT on November 2 for results and commentary.
